GMT

I've been playing with widgets and wanted to make a command center of sorts. So this is what I've come up with. The satellites being tracked are METEOR M2-4 and NOAA19. They* transmit in the 137Mhz band, and you can decode their weather data with an SDR! New post about how to do that soon hopefully.

*NOAA POES have been decommissioned as of 2025 sadly.
