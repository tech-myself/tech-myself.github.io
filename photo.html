My Gear!

I have two Cannon AE-1 Programs. A silver model and a black model. Two cameras allows me to shoot two film stocks simultaneously. Typically I'll run Kodak Gold 200ISO for color and Ilford HP5 Plus for black and white.

I was able to find a really cool 50mm 1984 olympic games lens to go with the black camera body. So it's set up like it would have been for the games back then.

Both cameras have 50mm lenses, then I also have one 70mm - 210mm BIG BOI zoom lens that is used on both cameras when needed.

This lens also has a really cool macro focus to "get right in there" for nice shots.
